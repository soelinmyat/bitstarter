Background Info

I'm Lin Myat. I'm from Myanmar. Myanmar is a country in South East Asia. You might have heard about it recently in the news. There was a recent political reform and the country is finally opening up. With that comes new oppotunities for start-ups.

One of the promising areas is tourism because Myanmar is filled with natural beauties and wonders which have been inaccessible to the outside world until recently. Myanmar is also listed as top 3 travel destination for 2012 by The New York Time.

Problem?

It's still relatively troublesome to plan a trip to Myanmar. For a start, you cannot use credit card or other online payments to pay for your trip. Even if you can, you run a risk of getting cheated by local agents. This is just the tip of the iceberg. As you plan for the trip, you will likely wonder if it is worth all the trouble.

What is MmTours?

MmTours is a one-stop platform for you to plan the trip to Myanmar. MmTours provide 3 main features.

Information. You will get all necessary information for your trips, including tourist attractions and travel advices through MmTours.

Easy planning. You'll be able to view available tour packages from tour agencies as you browse through attractions on MmTours. You'll interact with agents directly, buy packages and make payment through MmTours all in one place.

Credibility. All tour agencies will be reviewed thoroughly before they are allowed to list on MmTours. We will also get the endorsement from Myanmar Tourism Board to enhance our credibility.

What is included this Bitstarter project?

1. Development of MmTours platform.

2. Participation of at least 20 credible tour agencies.

3. Listing of at least 200 attractions

4. Listing of at least 100 tour packages
